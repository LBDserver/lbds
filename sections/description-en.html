LBDserver vocabulary: Description back to ToC

The LBDserver builds upon the Linked Data Platform and the Solid specifications for decentral data organisation. The LBDserver vocabulary adds to this in several ways:
  • Defining lbds:Aggregator as a specific instance of Solid-based LDP containers that may also aggregate (lbds:aggregates) resources and containers that are not stored in the pod of the aggregator. An aggregator can contain other aggregators, but eventually it groups instances of lbds:PartialProject, where stakeholders to an AEC project store their contributions.
  • Defining specific sub-properties for describing LBDserver registries, located in a lbds:PartialProject: lbds:DatasetRegistry, lbds:ReferenceRegistry and lbds:ServiceRegistry
  • Defining a structure to link sub-document identifiers in a free way, using a lbds:ReferenceRegistry. This structure largely bases upon the Information Container for Linked Document Delivery (ICDD, ISO 21597) but differs on important parts.
